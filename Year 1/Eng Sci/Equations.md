#### Power
---
$$
\displaylines{
P = V \times I \\
P = I^2 \times R \\
P =\frac{V^2}{R}
}
$$

#### Current
---
$$I = \frac{dq}{dt} = \frac{enAv \times dt}{dt} = enAv$$
$$I = \frac{V}{R}$$
#### Resistance
---
$$R = \frac{V}{I}$$$$R \propto \frac{l}{A}$$
$$R = \frac{\rho l}{A}$$
##### In Parallel
$$\frac{1}{R} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + ... + \frac{1}{R_n}$$
##### In Series
$$R = R_1 + R_2 + ... + R_n$$
#### Capacitors
---
$$ C = \frac{Q}{V} = \frac{dV}{dt}$$
